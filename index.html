About me

I am a population geneticist, currently employed as a full-time university assistant at the department of Botany & Biodiversity research, University of Vienna, Austria. My research is focused on the study of evolutionary forces: gene flow, drift, mutation and selection and their relative importance in divergence and speciation. I have particularly studied fungal, plant and animal models. My interests also include genomics - in its broader sense - and the statistical methods for population genetics.
